As well as being the authors and photographers of six guidebooks to date, we are routes editors for Trail Running magazine, columnists for Outdoor Fitness magazine and regular contributors to Runner's World, Trail and Country Walking magazines. We are Ordnance Survey GetOutside Champions and members of the Outdoor Writers and Photographers Guild and the Society of Authors. Our writing and photography has been published across numerous newspapers, websites, guidebooks and promotional materials. We are passionate about what we do fill our work with our enthusiasm and excitement for great adventures and amazing places.

We have a wealth of experience within the outdoor industry and work with a range of businesses and organisations to bring great adventures to beautiful places. We believe outdoor adventure should be sustainable, balancing recreation with conservation, supporting local communities and protecting precious places of all kinds. Jen has many years' clinical experience working with athletes of all abilities and is a qualified podiatrist with a MSc in sport and exercise medicine. Sim has a climbing and mountaineering background and is a qualified Leader in Running Fitness. He has worked as a leader for the British Exploring Society and has visited the Alps, Iceland and the Himalayas both with groups and on solo adventures. 

Our areas of expertise include off-road running, outdoor adventure, sports science, outdoor photography, endurance sport, family adventures, sustainability in outdoor recreation and navigation. We can provide:

  • Engaging, well-researched copy
  • Eye-catching outdoor adventure photography
  • Route design and mapping
  • Industry guidance, advice and expertise
  • Product testing, reviewing and development
  • Clothing and product photoshoots

Recent projects include:

  • National Trust Ranger Runs
  • Running with the Forestry Commission
  • The South West Coast Path Association's 40for40 campaign
  • Photoshoot showcasing Aussie Grit apparel for Women's Running magazine award
  • The Wildlife Trusts Every Child Wild campaign
