We have a wealth of experience within the outdoor industry and work with local, national and international businesses and organisations to bring great adventures to beautiful places. We believe adventures should be sustainable, balancing recreation with conservation, supporting local communities and inspiring others to take a considerate approach to outdoor adventure.

We have written and photographed six guidebooks to date and we're currently working on our next. We are routes editors at Trail Running magazine, write a monthly column and feature for Outdoor Fitness magazine and contribute regularly to Runner's World, Trail and Country Walking magazines. We've been Ordnance Survey GetOutside Champions since 2016 and hold membership of both the Outdoor Writers and Photographers Guild and the Society of Authors. Our writing and photography has been published in a range of newspapers, websites, guidebooks and promotional materials and we regularly deliver talks, workshops and guided runs at festivals and events. We are passionate about what we do and our enthusiasm and knowledge is reflected in every project we undertake.

We are experienced endurance athletes with well over 100 marathons and ultras completed between us, as well as Ironman triathlons, adventure races, multi-day bikepacking, fastpacking and climbing trips. Jen has many years' clinical experience working with athletes of all abilities and is a qualified podiatrist with an MSc in sport and exercise medicine. Sim graduated in environmental earth science and has a climbing and mountaineering background. He is a qualified Leader in Running Fitness and has worked as a leader for the British Exploring Society. He has run, mountain biked and climbed around the country and explored the Alps, Iceland and the Himalayas both with groups and on solo adventures. 

Our areas of expertise include off-road running, outdoor adventure, sports science, outdoor photography, endurance sport, navigation, family adventures and sustainability in outdoor recreation. We can provide:

  • Engaging, well-researched copy
  • Eye-catching outdoor adventure photography
  • Route design and mapping
  • Industry guidance, advice and expertise
  • Product testing, reviewing and development
  • Clothing and product photoshoots

Recent projects include:

  • Consultancy, research, copywriting, mapping and photography for the National Trust Ranger Runs project
  • Consultancy, research, copywriting, mapping and photography for the Forestry Commission's Wild Running routes
  • Ambassadors for the South West Coast Path Association's 40for40 campaign
  • Modelling and photoshoot for Aussie Grit Apparel
  • The Wildlife Trusts' Every Child Wild campaign

If you'd like to chat to us about a forthcoming project we'd love to hear from you.
